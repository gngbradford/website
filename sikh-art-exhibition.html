Sunday, 17 July 2016

Sikh Art Exhibition

It was a great pleasure to host the opening of the Khalsa Fitness – Sikh Art Exhibition on Saturday 18th of June 2016. The event was held at the Khalsa Fitness Gym which is located at Guru Nanak Gurdwara, Usher Street, Bradford.

The exhibition included works by many artists, some local, national and international. All exhibits have been carefully chosen to inspire and spiritually enlighten the sangat on Guruji’s message and tell the story of our great Sikh history.

The artworks on display are all very unique and inspiring, we are sure that they will be enjoyed and will provoke a sense of pride and emotion, along with educating us all in our rich history.

On the launch day we will had some of the artists talking about what inspired them to take up art and sharing with us the inspiration and the message of their fantastic work.
